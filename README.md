# Supermarket ERP System Showcase

## Overview

A custom Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system designed and deployed to support the daily operations of a retail supermarket.
The system was created to replace manual inventory tracking, supplier management, purchasing workflows, and operational reporting with a centralized digital platform.
Currently used to manage over 1,500 products and streamline multiple business processes within a live retail environment.

## Key Features
### Inventory Management
* Product creation and editing
* Stock level monitoring
* Inventory adjustments
* Low-stock visibility

### Supplier Management
* Supplier database management
* Purchase tracking
* Supplier transaction history

### Purchase & Stock Control
* Purchase order recording
* Incoming stock tracking
* Inventory updates and synchronization

### Sales Analytics
* Revenue monitoring
* Product performance analysis
* Sales reporting dashboards

### Reporting
* Inventory reports
* Purchase reports
* Business performance insights
